About

The Municipality of Tukuran, located in the province of Zamboanga del Sur, is a thriving coastal town known for its abundant natural resources and vibrant agricultural economy. Blessed with rich fishing grounds and fertile lands, Tukuran has long been recognized for its bountiful marine harvest and flourishing rice production. The presence of rice mills throughout the municipality further supports the livelihood of local farmers, making agriculture one of the primary sources of income for many Tukuranons. Fishing, farming, and rice milling not only sustain the community’s daily needs but also contribute significantly to the town’s economic growth. With its strategic location and hardworking people, Tukuran continues to develop while preserving the traditions that have long sustained its identity as a hub of rich agricultural and coastal resources.

Chemical Drayer

An open-air structure used for rice drying facility. It is built with metals and concrete with small openings used for ventilations. A chemical dryer for rice fields is a specialized structure designed for efficient moisture removal from harvested rice grains. Unlike traditional sun drying, which is slow and weather-dependent, chemical dryers offer a controlled environment to accelerate the process. This dryer, with precise temperature and humidity control, ensures consistent and high-quality drying. The use of chemicals, such as certain gases, can further enhance drying efficiency and prevent spoilage. Chemical dryers are more expensive to build and operate than traditional methods but offer significant advantages in terms of speed, yield, and grain quality, particularly crucial in areas with unpredictable weather patterns. This results in reduced post-harvest losses and improved market value..
